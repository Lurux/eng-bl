Scuba diving !

John, August 8, 2017

I just did scuba diving yesterday! Marine life experts had to check the corals and wildlife activity, so I decided to come with them to learn more about the island, as this is my weekly day off. The seabed are exceptional, especially with the corals variety. I learnt a lot about the local wildlife and I discovered many endemic species, it interests me a lot!

 Also, after seeing closely the Australian’s ecosystem and, the Great Barrier Reef, I just flown over it to see how huge and beautiful they were. The view from airplane is simply stunning! I have seen through the crystal-dear water a large range of animals and floral species. Now I can study them if I want to help the experts ^^

Visiting the island !

John, July 23, 2017

Today was just my first working day, and I visited every nook of the island. There was a wooden bridge to repair, and I tinkered with branches around restore the rail…

I watered the plants near the golf because the climate is very hot and dry here – there may be one full month without rain at Hamilton’s! – and seen all the dangerous or isolated beaches to guard… The sun is actually really hot here so we have to drink a lot, that’s why I also proposed adding more fountains for the swimmers.

At midday to celebrate my arrival I just ate at The Pavilion, one of the bests restaurants of the island, with some other staff of here. There were garlic lobsters with local vegetables, it was all simply delicious!

My first day here !

John, July 21, 2017

Okay, I just arrived to the island yesterday at night and the landscape is definitely outstanding! White sand beaches, blue sky, warm crystal-clear water and amazing scuba-diving spots…

There’s even a golf course just a hundred feet away from there! The villa is simply outstanding, luxurious, modern and has all the comfort you can dream of! There’s a private swimming pool, with an outside bar just nearby! The rooms are very spacious with a wonderful view on the sea, and the kitchen is fully equipped.

Since the jetlag quite tired me, I just had a rest in the hammock near the Jacuzzi just before writing this!

It’ll cetrainly be hard working instead of enjoying this paradise! I’ve even seen koalas in the trees, if I have had my camera, I would have taken a picture for you ;)
